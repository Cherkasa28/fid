About Her

Sarah Cherkaoui is a student at Seminole State College working towards her Associates in Science for Graphic Design.
She has a background as a self-taught artist, taking school classes to help her refine her work. She has been interested in art from a young age. She aspires to be a character concept artist in the video game industry. She would also like to pursue having a web comic as a passion project. She wants to learn how to make 3d models and incorporate them into her work, as well as learn to animate in 2d.
